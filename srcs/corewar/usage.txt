Usage: ./corewar [(-dump NBR_CYCLE | -v) -p] [-n VALUE -a VALUE] <champion1.cor> [...] <...>
-dump		: Choose the number of the cycle to print
			  Must be > 0
-v			: Open the visualizer
-p 			: Show players details
-n			: Choose the player number
			  Must be != 0  (uint_max - number of players)
-a			: Choose the place of the champion
